State Education Summary Report for MD

13 October, 2022

Overview

Using high school and ACT data from the National Center for Educational Statistics (NCES), a report was created to describe the state of Marylands education systems.

  • A table (Table 1) of descriptive high-school statistics for Maryland. These can be compared with the following national averages:
    • The average number of teachers reported in U.S. high schools is 55 teachers.
    • The average number of students reported in U.S. high schools is 907 students.
    • The average student:teacher ratio reported in U.S. high schools is 16 students to teachers.
  • Charts (Figures 1-5) of Marylands ACT scores.
